Wavelength

January 2017

GGJ 19

This is the second game jam I have participated in. The team consisted of five people: four programmers, one of whom was me, and a friend who did the art for us. "Waves" was the theme that year, which lead to electromagnetic waves, which led to making a puzzle game called Wavelength. The game is built around navigating mazes. The player can change the layout of the maze by interacting with "transmitters". What came out was a compelling puzzle game which uncharacteristically for game jams even had a tutorial. All in all - 48 hours well spent.
